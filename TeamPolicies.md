# Team Policy

Designated team roles:
- Jennifer Huang as Coordinator
- Yan Chi Ng as Monitor
- Sungeun Kim as Checker

1. We will plan and communicate technical aspects of the project primarly through GitLab issues.

2. Our secondary form of communications will be via Discord.

3. We are given 24 hours to respond to team messages on GitLab and other platforms.

4. Our common meeting time will be on Friday afternoons. We will have at least one in-person meeting per week.

5. We are to show up to every team meeting unless we have notified other teammate of prior obligations.

6. If there are emergencies/reasons where one of us cannot complete our work on time, we will notify the team as soon as possible. We will also give regular updates on the situation.

7. If a teammate does not respond/is unreachable for 2 days, the rest of the team may report to the manager/instructor.

8. If there are disagreements and conflicts, we will handle it within the team first before asking help from the manager/instructor.

9. If a teammate is struggling with a task, they will seek help from others, and their teammates are willing to assist when asked
